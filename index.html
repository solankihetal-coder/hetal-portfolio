A bit more about me

I'm a designer who codes, or a developer who designs, depends on the day. I spent about a year working as a UI/UX designer, picked up frontend development along the way so I could ship what I imagined, and now I move between Figma and VS Code without feeling like I've switched jobs

Today, I'm taking that experience into freelance work designing the experience, then building it myself, so there's no gap between the idea and the interface.

Went Hybrid
